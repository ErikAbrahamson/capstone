Pavlov 

A task management app that keeps you accountable

Real Accountability

Humans are inherently selfish, and often times that means procrastinating is a better choice than buckling down and taking care of a task. There are a lot of task managers out there, but there aren't many that hold you responsible by taking action in your favor with a negative consequence.

Pavlov will take care of this for you. Consider it a little helper wielding a stun-stick to poke you when you fail to do something important.
